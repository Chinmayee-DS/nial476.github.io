Tactile Perception for Robot Grasping and Manipulation

Exploring the use of tactile perception for robotic manipulation in simulation (Tacto) and real life (UR5e). We leverage the high spatial resolution of the GelSight sensor by capturing tactile images of chess pieces for classification and board placement task.

Localization using Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF)

Using Neural Radiance Fields for robot localization in an environment. Using traditional Particle Filter and NeRF as a 3D map of the environment to find the location and orientation of a novel camera view.
